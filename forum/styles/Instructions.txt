Installing
---------------------------

- Upload the "X-Treme" folder to your styles directory.

- Visit your ACP > Styles section and install the style



Editing
---------------------------

- Check the "Extras" folder for Photoshop .psd files that you may edit to your liking.



Extras
---------------------------

- Styles may include an "Extras" folder with matching avatars, ranks, or various graphics.

- If these aren't present you may visit www.black-fusion.com/forum/ and post a request in the appropriate forum and one of my staff or myself might be able to get them for you.
